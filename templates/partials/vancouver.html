Getting to the Wedding Venue

The Burnaby Village Museum is located in Burnaby, British Columbia. It is about 30 minutes east from Vancouver city center

If you are planning to drive, there is free parking onsite. If you plan to take Uber or transit, there are a few options that require Skytrain and Bus. We recommend planning your trip in advance and can use the Translink site.

The main entrance of our venue is located just off Highway 1 at Canada Way and Deer Lake Avenue.

Please do not drink and drive, plan accordingly and have a safe ride home.
