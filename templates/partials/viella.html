Travelling to Viella, France

Viella is located in the beautiful Ger region of southwest France.

For those traveling into Viella, there are a number of airports to fly into. We recommend arriving via either Bordeaux-Mérignac Airport (BOR) or Tarbes-Lourdes-Pyrénées Airport (LDE). Alternatively there is a high speed train from Paris to Bordeaux (~2 hour journey). Veilla is a 2 hour drive from Bordeaux and a 45 minute drive from Lordes.

We can't wait to welcome you to the southwest of France! Here are a few recommendations of our favourite places to visit while you're here.

  • Restaurant: Restaurant de la Tour
  • Day trip: Cauterets
  • Place to relax: Eugénie-les-Bains
  • Local jazz festival: Jazz in Marciac

Getting to the Wedding Venue

The Chateau Viella is located on the eastern side of Viella and is walking distance to the center of the village.

There is free parking available onsite.

Please do not drink and drive, plan accordingly and have a safe ride home.

Where to Stay

As Viella is a village of only a couple hundred residents, most places to stay will likely be in a nearby village. Below is a brief list of recommendations on places to stay near (or in) Viella. We are happy to answer any questions or make suggestions for individuals or groups.

La Villa Du Bearn La Villa Du Bearn is located about 16km away in Garlin.
Le Prielle Le Prielle is located in Madiran. Minimum booking of 7 nights.
Hotels/Bed and Breakfasts Around Town There are a number of smaller hotels and B&B's in and around Viella.
Vacation rentals Both VRBO and airbnb have a number of vacation rentals in the area. This is also a highly recommended and often more affordable option for those who don't mind something a bit more self-service.
